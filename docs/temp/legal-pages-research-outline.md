**CCPA Threshold Analysis for Home Wise Realty Group:**
As a regional Florida brokerage, HWRG likely does not meet the $26.6M revenue threshold
or the 100,000 California records threshold. However, including CCPA-style rights language
costs nothing, signals trustworthiness, and protects against future growth crossing thresholds.

**Florida Digital Bill of Rights (SB 262) Analysis:**
The comprehensive consumer rights provisions of SB 262 only apply to companies with over

requirement that any for-profit entity conducting business in Florida must obtain consumer
consent before selling sensitive personal data DOES apply. Since HWRG does not sell personal
data, this requires only a disclosure confirming that.
